Various Educational Math Resources

Hello, I graduated from Amherst College in 2022 with a degree in math and a strong interest in teaching. Over the years, I wrote a bunch of documents on assorted undergraduate math topics, the most extensive being a text on branches of complex functions and integration. I hope people will find these resources helpful, as they were written with education in mind. If you notice any errors, feel free to email me at wtseng9@gmail.com. Please do download the documents onto your computer, they are much easier to read that way!

Enjoy!
