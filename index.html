Various Educational Math Resources

Hello, I graduated from Amherst College in 2022 with a degree in math and a strong interest in teaching. Over the years, I wrote a bunch of documents on assorted undergraduate math topics, the most extensive being a text on branches of complex functions and integration. I hope people will find these resources helpful, as they were written with education in mind. If you notice any errors, let me know at wtseng9@gmail.com. Thank you to Reinis Mazeiks for making this website prettier, and thank you to all my math professors, including Professor Zhang, Daniels, D. Benedetto, and many more...

Enjoy!
